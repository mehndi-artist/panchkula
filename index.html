Frequently Asked Questions

Is the home service completely free?

Yes. We do not charge any travel fees for our primary service areas including Panchkula, Chandigarh, Mohali, and Zirakpur. You only pay for the mehndi package you select.

How long does the bridal mehndi application take?

Depending on the level of detail and coverage, a detailed bridal package typically takes between three to six hours.

How early should I book my dates?

We highly recommend booking two to three months in advance to secure your preferred dates, especially during the peak wedding season.

Do you bring your own supplies?

Absolutely. We bring our fresh, organic henna cones, a lemon-sugar sealant mixture, and all necessary application tools to your location.
